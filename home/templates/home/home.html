Who we are

Study Buddies hopes to assist others in finding their own study buddies. Due to COVID-19, socializing with peers is difficult. As college students ourselves, we understand how isolating and challenging online learning can be. Zoom Univeristy has taken away more natural, in person opportunities for you and your classmates to interact before, during, and after class. Have no fear! Study Buddies is here. We hope to help other people find their true study buddy or buddies. We strongly believe in upholding the University of Virginia's Honor System. We urge our users to promote a virtual community of trust on this platform. Be kind and supportive to your fellow study buddies. Good luck studying! You've got this!
